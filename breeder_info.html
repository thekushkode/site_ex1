VHE Rottweilers features some of the top German & Serbian Rottweiler bloodlines. VHE has been showing thier dogs since 2010 and have earned countless awards. All puppies are bred with the dogs safety and well-being in mind. This breeder does not crop the Rottweiler's tail. This practice is considered more humane but is also against the AKC's breed description. Rottweilers make great family pets. They are extremely loyal, good with kids, and have a natural protection instinct.
